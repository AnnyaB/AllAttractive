Can Machines Truly Become Conscious?

  1. Fundamental Question: As Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) evolves, a key question emerges— What distinguishes true consciousness from computational algorithms?
  2. Vedic Insight: The Vedas clarify this distinction through the science of the Ātma (soul):
    • The ātma is eternal, conscious, and blissful.
    • It is distinct from the body, brain, and mind.
    • Consciousness originates from the soul, not from matter.
  3. Philosophical Relevance: Vedic epistemology explains what modern AI theory struggles with—subjective awareness, intention, and moral agency.
  4. Srila Prabhupāda's Clarity: He taught that life is not a product of chemicals or code but of the living soul (jīva), which no machine can replicate.
"The living entity is not the body, nor the brain, but the eternal spiritual soul." — Bhagavad Gītā 2.20
