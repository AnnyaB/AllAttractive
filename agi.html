Artificial Intelligence & The Soul

Can machines become conscious? What distinguishes a self-aware soul from an algorithm? Vedic epistemology answers these crucial questions by differentiating the atma (self) from the body and mind.

"The living entity is not the body, nor the brain, but the eternal spiritual soul." — Bhagavad Gita 2.20

As AGI evolves, understanding consciousness becomes vital. Srila Prabhupada clearly explained that soul and matter are fundamentally different — a truth even AI scientists are beginning to grasp.
