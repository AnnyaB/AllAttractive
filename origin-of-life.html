Origin of Life: Accident or Intelligence?

  1. The Central Question: Is life merely a chemical accident — the product of chance and matter — or is it the outcome of an intelligent, divine design?
  2. Vedic Assertion: Ancient Vedic science emphatically declares: Life comes from life, not from dead matter.
  3. Scriptural Foundation:
    "janmādy asya yataḥ" — Vedanta Sutra 1.1.1
    "The Absolute Truth is that from which everything emanates."
    Life, consciousness, intelligence — all originate from a supreme, sentient source: Śrī Kṛṣṇa.
  4. Scientific Gaps: Modern biology struggles to explain:
    • The origin of DNA’s information content.
    • The emergence of consciousness from inert molecules.
    • The teleological (purpose-driven) nature of biological systems.
  5. Vedic Perspective on Design: Just as a coded language requires a mind, the intricate genetic code within living organisms points to a conscious designer — not randomness.
  6. Kṛṣṇa Consciousness: This movement uniquely bridges spiritual philosophy and scientific thought, offering a coherent framework for the origin, purpose, and destiny of life.
