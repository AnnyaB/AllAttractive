Philosophy & the Ultimate Reality

  1. Timeless Questions: Humanity’s greatest inquiries have always been: What is truth? What is the self? Who is God?
  2. Vedic Response: The Vedic scriptures offer a complete framework to answer these questions through ontology, epistemology, and theology:
    • The self (ātma) is eternal, conscious, and blissful.
    • Truth is not just an abstract ideal — it is a Person: Śrī Kṛṣṇa, the Absolute Truth (satyaṁ param).
    • God is knowable through love (bhakti), not just reason.
  3. Cross-Cultural Harmony: The Vedic and Biblical revelations align on fundamental metaphysical insights:
    • "In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God..." — John 1:1
    • "Om pūrṇam adaḥ pūrṇam idam..." — Iśopaniṣad: Both describe the completeness and omnipresence of the Divine.
  4. Path to Realization: Through Bhakti-yoga and transcendental knowledge (jñāna), one goes beyond material illusion and realizes the eternal self and the Supreme Being.
