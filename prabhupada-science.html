Guru Parampara & Acharyas

The disciplic succession (Guru Parampara) is the backbone of Vedic wisdom transmission. Srila Prabhupada belongs to the Brahma-Madhva-Gaudiya Sampradaya.

The original receiver of the Vedas from Lord Krishna. He initiated the line of disciplic succession.

The divine sage who spread bhakti-yoga and instructed Vyasa Deva.

The compiler of the Vedic scriptures, including the Mahabharata and Srimad Bhagavatam.

Propounder of Dvaita philosophy and restorer of pure devotional teachings.

Preached the chanting of the holy names and established the yuga-dharma for Kali Yuga.

The Founder-Acharya of ISKCON who brought Krishna Consciousness to the West and founded the Bhaktivedanta Institute.
